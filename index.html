How It Works

How an (EDA) Sensor Works:

  • Electrodes on the Skin: Detects changes in sweat gland activity caused by autonomic nervous system activity.
  • Measuring Skin Conductance: Increased sweat causes higher electrical flow, measured in microsiemens (µS).
  • Signal Processing: Algorithms identify patterns linked to seizures.
  • Output and Interpretation: Displays data or triggers alerts for effective monitoring.
